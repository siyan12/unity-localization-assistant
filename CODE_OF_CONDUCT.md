# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education,
socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, assume good intent while discussing evidence, give and accept
constructive feedback, acknowledge mistakes, and prioritize the health of the
community. Harassment, threats, sexualized attention, discriminatory language,
doxing, and sustained disruption are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may issue a
warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban when behavior is inappropriate. Scope
includes project spaces and public representation of the project.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository maintainers' GitHub
contact channel. Do not disclose personal or sensitive details in a public
issue. Reports will be reviewed promptly, fairly, and as confidentially as
practical; retaliation is prohibited.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
